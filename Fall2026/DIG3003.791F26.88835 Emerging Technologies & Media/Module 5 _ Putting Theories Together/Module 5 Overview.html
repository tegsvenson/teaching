Module 5 Overview

Through the past few weeks, we have reviewed how our cognitive system is limited, what media effects mean to understanding user experiences, and what are practical the principles and considerations when it comes to designing an effective user interface. In this module, we put everything together in one place and see how these concepts are interconnected, and using that idea, come up with an idea for your project assignment.

 

 Module Learning Outcome

Upon successful completion of this module, you will be able to:

  1. Identify the major drivers of technological advancement and how it affects the way people communicate.
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Discussion, (2) Assignment;
  2. List the ethical and legal implications of communication technologies.
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Discussion, (2) Assignment;
  3. Identify problems and solutions regarding technology in communication. 
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Discussion, (2) Assignment;
