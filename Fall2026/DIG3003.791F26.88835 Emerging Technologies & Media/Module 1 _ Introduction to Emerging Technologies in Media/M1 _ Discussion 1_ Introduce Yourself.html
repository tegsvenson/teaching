M1 _ Discussion 1_ Introduce Yourself

You, Yourself, and Your Medium

For this discussion, you are writing a self-introduction. We want to learn how other people may use media differently through this discussion. 

 Introduce Yourself

First, you need to incorporate the following elements into your introduction:

1. General information about yourself (e.g., your name, major, interests, etc.).

2. One interesting fact about yourself (to keep the conversation interesting).

3. Identify and explain one application or medium that you use most frequently, focusing on how you may use the application differently than others. Share at least one unique thing that you do with the application. Discuss why you use it, how it benefits you, and how it has influenced your daily life.

The deadline for this part is August 28, 2025.

 Respond to a Classmate

Next, after you have submitted your introduction, you will be expected to read the introductions of your classmates and choose one to comment on.

1. Your response should be either

A. An alternative app that you use to achieve the same outcome as your classmate's use case. Explain the process and the outcome using the alternative app involves in detail. For full credit, also provide your thoughts on how these different apps may differ in terms of the outcomes and the overall experience (i.e., provide pros & cons).

   or,

B. Share how your experience differs from your classmates on the same app. Explain in detail how you differently use the same app. For full credit, also explain how the effect our outcome may differ between your use case and your classmate's, focusing on the predicted advantages and the disadvantages.

2. Only the first reply counts. Subsequent comments will not count as full participation. This is so that everyone receives a reply from their classmate. E-mail me if there is no suitable post for you to reply.

3. The application/media/platform/device from your classmate to whom you are leaving a reply should be different from the application/media/platform/device that you discussed in your original post.

The deadline for this part is August 31, 2025.
