Module # | Lesson #: Removing Clicks and Pops

  Lectures

When working physically close to microphones, such as on a podcast, it is very easy for the microphone to pick up unwanted sounds. Our lips smack, tongues click, and mouths pop. In this lesson, you will learn how to quickly remove clicks and pops from your audio.
