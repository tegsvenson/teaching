Module 7 Overview

Algorithm is arguably the cream of the crop for both social media and social media marketing. As information on social media is distributed based on algorithms, piggybacking your message on the algorithms will send our brand's message to more people more effectively. With ideas about algorithms on Lessons 12 and 13, you will work on your own strategy to devise a plan to take advantage of the algorithms in Assignment 4. We are concluding the theoretical and strategic phase of social media marketing campaign in this module.
