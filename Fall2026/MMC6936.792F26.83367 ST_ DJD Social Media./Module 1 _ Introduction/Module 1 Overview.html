Welcome

When I say "Understanding the Beast," no, I'm not talking about Mr. Beast. Rather, I mean the beast, the mega-monster that is social media in today's society. Before we can start attempting to wrangle this monster and use it in communicative settings, we need to understand how social media came to be and best practices --ethical, legal, and journalistic-- to approach it for professional use. 

  Module Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will:

  • MO1: Identify the history, evolution, and current practices of social media in journalism and communication-related professions 
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Quiz, (2) Discussion
  • MO2: Learn how to uphold social media content to traditional journalistic and communication norms and ethics, such as fact-checking, verification, accuracy, objectivity, ethics, etc.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Quiz, (2) Discussion
  • MO3: Understand the ethical and legal complexities, including privacy concerns, posed to journalism and communication professionals while using social media.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Quiz, (2) Discussion
