Module 9 Overview

We are finally launching our brand's social media marketing campaign in this module. We will first learn about the basic ideas regarding creating content and running livestreams in Lesson 15. Then, we will work on creating a stack of content before the campaign launches so we can post them following our schedule. Finally, there is always room for improvement when it comes to marketing campaigns. By working on Assignment 8, we will look back at our campaign plan and see if there is anything that we can improve.

  Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will:

  • MO1: Identify the optimal guidelines and practices for content creation on social media, including live streaming, content curation, and multimedia production.

  • MO2: Explain effective strategies for social media branding promotion.
