Module 4 Overview

The module discusses social networks in the context of social media marketing. We delve into the fundamentals of social network analysis and utilizing social cognition for persuasion. We discuss strategies for gaining more followers and enhancing engagement, emphasizing the significance of connecting with an interested audience. Lastly, we touch on the automatic processes of feeling close to others and anthropomorphism in perceiving brands, leading to our conclusion for this module. We also start working on our social media marketing campaign this week with Assignment 1: Social Media Audit.
