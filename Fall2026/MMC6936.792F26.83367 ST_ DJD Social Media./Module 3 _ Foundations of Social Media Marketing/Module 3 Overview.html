Module 3 Overview

Module 3 | Overview 

This module covers the theoretical foundations of social media marketing, delving into the evolution of social media and the transition from Web 1.0 to Web 2.0. We will focus on how the evolution of social media marketing is intertwined with advancements in social media platforms. As a result of this relationship, we will learn what makes social media work and also makes social media marketing work. The focus also is on understanding various communication theories rooted in social psychology as crucial elements to understanding social media marketing strategies. Continuing from last week’s discussion, in this week’s discussion, each of us will select the brand to work on for this semester.
