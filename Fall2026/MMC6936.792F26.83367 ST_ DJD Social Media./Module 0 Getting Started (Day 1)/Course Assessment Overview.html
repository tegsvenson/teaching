 Social Media Marketing Project

The Assignments:

The main assignment of this semester is the Social Media Marketing Project. As one of the core objectives of this course, you will learn how to plan, research, and execute a marketing plan for a brand on social media. Throughout this series of assignments, you will plan for a marketing campaign on social media and execute your plan on one of them. Since it is practically impossible for everybody to run a marketing campaign on an actual brand without legal/ethical complications, we are creating hypothetical brands for our assignment. We are planning and running a social media marketing campaign on a platform that you select. You are asked to create a brand account on a social media platform of your choice and make postings on it. You can find the related assignments in the "Assignments" menu.

Here are the assignments with a very brief description for each of them:

  • Discussion 2 & 3: We come up with an idea for a brand for your classmate to work on; then, pick a brand that others proposed and work on it throughout this semester.
  • Social Media Audit: We survey what's happening on social media in general, related to our brand.
  • Audience, Personas, & Goals: We investigate who on social media would be interested in knowing our brand.
  • Branding Profile: We create the theme for our brand based on what we found about the audience.
  • Algorithm & SEO Strategies: Learn about the function of algorithms on social media; and how we can leverage it through search engine optimization.
  • Content Guidelines: Create a unified theme for our brand.
  • Content Production: Prepare 10 posts and a live stream for our social media.
  • Editorial Calendar: Create a timeline for your posts before you start your campaign.
  • Finalized Marketing & Content Plan: Fix any issues from "Social Media Audit" to "Content Production," and check if everything is good to go before you start posting.
  • Post: Create and prepare posts according to your plan.
  • Analytics & Revenue Potential Report: Your marketing campaign for your target audience is over, and you analyze your performance and compare it to others.

Assignments 1 to 8 are the preparation stage; 9 is the execution stage; and 10 is the post hoc review stage.

 

How to Prepare for Your Project

You will be provided with a template for each assignment. Starting from the first assignment, you will create a single project file that contains all assignments. This project file will live online, most likely on Canva, since that is the tool that I use to share the templates. For each assignment, you submit this project file in both online (hyperlink) and offline (PDF) format. For example, your submission of Assignment 3 should incorporate your Assignments 1, 2, and 3.

 

How Social Media Marketing Projects are Graded:

As you can see here, the assignments sequentially stack on top of their previous ones. That means if you have an issue with one of them, you may have the same issue for most of the subsequent assignments. For example, if you make a mistake on the Audience, Personas, & Goals assignment and miscalculate your target audience, the rest of your campaign will fail to reach your target.

I will provide feedback for each step, from Assignment 1 to 7, to prevent this domino effect. You will receive notes from me if there are issues in your assignments, and you are asked to address the issues. Each issue should be addressed ideally before the next assignment starts, but we are all very busy. Therefore, Assignment 8, "Finalized Marketing & Content Plan," is dedicated to fixing the issues before you start posting.

Consider Assignment 8 as your mid-term paper.I aim to be quite critical about your work up until Assignment 7. You may receive harsher grades than usual for these assignments. Do not worry. I allocated only small portions of your final grade to these assignments. You will have a chance to recover the points that you lost (if you lost) by Assignment 8. Late work, missing the assignment completely, or an original assignment that scores less than 45% /(this is hard to achieve) are excluded from the grade adjustment for Assignment 8.

Note 1: You have to finish all seven previous assignments and receive my feedback to start working on Assignment 8, no matter how late your submission is. 

Note 2: Working on a random brand that is not your pick in Discussion 3 results in 15% point penalty for all submitted project assignments.

 Hootsuite Social Marketing Certification

WHAT IS HOOTSUITE?:

Hootsuite is one of the most popular social media monitoring and management platforms in the world. It's used by professionals in journalism, PR, marketing, and other communication fields. Pretty much anyone who does any work in social media is familiar with Hootsuite. Hootsuite created an education site called Hootsuite Academy to educate communication professionals on 1) how to use Hootsuite, and 2) some aspects of using social media.

WHAT IS A HOOTSUITE CERTIFICATION?:

A professional certification shows consumers and potential employers that you are well-trained. It gives them confidence in your abilities and knowledge. Certification makes you more valuable to employers.

As part of this course, you can obtain two certifications for free:

  • Hootsuite Platform Certification($99 value) --optional
    • Hootsuite is one of the most popular platforms for social media management; it's the Microsoft Office of social media. It's well known in the industry, and this certification provides a tangible skillset you can offer to potential employers.
  • Social Marketing Certification($199 value) --mandatory
    • This certification is an exam that shows you have expertise in social media marketing, and understand concepts such as branding, strategy, media analytics, etc. It is something to add to your resume if you pass the certification exam.

To obtain the certifications, you will take the two courses (Hootsuite Platform) and (Social Marketing), and then take the certification exams for each. You can take each exam for free up to three times. If you pass, you obtain your certification. If you don't pass, you can continue to take it but Hootsuite will ask you to pay (beyond those first three paid attempts).

IS THE CERTIFICATION RELATED TO MY GRADE?:

Yes, Taking the Social Marketing Certification exam is required for this class. It is worth 10% of the grade. (The Hootsuite platform certification is optional, though).

To earn the certification, you need to score at least 80% on the exam. You'll get to take the exam for free up to 3 times.

Note that you are credited for your work in this course during this semester. That is, to receive credit for the Hootsuite assignment, you need to acquire this certification within this semester. If you acquired the certification before the beginning of this semester, email me to receive a substitute assignment.

WHAT DO I NEED TO DO?:

First, you should follow the detailed instructions in this document on how to sign up for free. (There is also information here on an alternative assignment if you happen to already have your Hootsuite Social Marketing certification). Then, within the next 4 weeks, you'll be working through that certification based on the recommended schedule I post every week. If you want to work ahead, feel free to do so!
