Module 15 | Finalize Project + Reflection Paper

Throughout the class, you've been working on the individual parts of a comprehensive social media strategy plan! For this final reflection, you'll be putting together your work in a way that creates a tangible strategy document for your portfolio, and reflecting on your journey.

  Assignment Instructions

1. Beautification

You should go through all of the social media strategy documents you've created from this class and "beautify" them.

  • Clean them up, and apply your branding profile to each section.
  • Make sure that your headings are consistent, that you use colors and fonts appropriately, and that you place your brand logo in the correct places. 
  • Assignments 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 9 (and 10, if you completed it) should be included in that order.

2. Make a Cover

Make a cover page for your strategy plan! You can do so by creating a file in Canva. The cover page should:

  • Be 8.5 inches by 11 inches, which is the standard letter paper size.
  • Include your group brand name and logo.
  • Have a title, "Social Media Strategy," for example.
  • Put your name.

3. Compile

Once you've finished cleaning up all of your documents, export them as PDFs and save them in the same folder on your computer. Then, combine each of them into one document. You must have already been doing this if you were following the course. Here is how to compile them anyway:

  • You can use Canva's tools, which you can arrive after passing through the "Pro" subscription prompt and comes with watermarks (which is completely fine for this course's purposes).
  • Or, you can use Adobe Acrobat or this tool.

4. Reflect

After you have finished your social media strategy portfolio document, write a paragraph reflection. Formal writing, preferably with APA or AP style, is required.

Here you answer the following questions:

  • Would you say your experience with this brand strategy was successful? Why or why not?
  • If you would start this strategy over, what would you do differently? 
  • What skills have you learned from this social media strategy experience that will be helpful to you in your professional career?

5. Turn it In

 Turn in a single PDF document that includes both project assignment series and your reflection paragraph.

Deadline: by 11:59 p.m. on December 11, 2025. No late submissions will be accepted for this assignment since it is the end of the term.
