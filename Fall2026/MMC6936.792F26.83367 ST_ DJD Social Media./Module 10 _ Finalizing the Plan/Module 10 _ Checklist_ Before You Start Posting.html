Module 9 | Checklist: Before You Start Posting

 Assignment Checklist

Before we begin, let's make sure that we have everything ready. We should be ready with the following:

  An account for our brand, which is designed following our Branding Profile and Content Guidelines.

  A series of content either ready to be posted or planned to be created soon, which matches your posting schedule.

  A finished Assignment 8 before you start posting. Your planning might have had some issues to address, so make sure that they are addressed. Assignment 9 and Final Assignment grading is based on how closely you followed your plan, which includes modifications as per the instructor feedback (i.e., Assignment 8).

DO NOT START POSTING if you think you are not ready with any of these. Remember, your postings are graded, and the grades are based on how accurately you followed your planning.
