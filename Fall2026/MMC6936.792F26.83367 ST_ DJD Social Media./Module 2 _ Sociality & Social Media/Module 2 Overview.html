Module 2 Overview

Module 2: Overview

This module introduces functional and morphological definitions of the concept of social media. We will extend its meaning to a social phenomenon rather than merely a technological one, where the primary mechanism involves people gathering and interacting online. The course will explore social media marketing in the upcoming lessons. We will also propose our brands in the discussion assignment.

  Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will:

  • MO1: Define what is Social Media.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Quiz, (2) Discussion
  • MO2: Identify social interaction and analytic framework related to social media.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Quiz, (2) Discussion
  • MO3: Identify how marketing and advertising affect social media companies. 
    •  Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Quiz, (2) Discussion
