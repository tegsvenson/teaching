Modules 11, 12, & 13 Overview

Over the next three weeks, in Modules 11 to 13, you will execute your plan. All your content should be ready to be posted now. This means your task is fairly simple. Go ahead and post. You will also pursue the Hootsuite certification during this time. Then, in Assignment 9, we will archive what we created and posted on social media by putting all the posts we made together in one document. 

  Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will:

  • M01: Identify the best guidelines and good practices of content production for social media, including using SM for live streaming, content curation, multimedia production, brand promotion, and news.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Assignment
  • M0 2: Comprehend the ethical and legal complexities, including privacy concerns, that are posed to journalism and communication professionals while using social media.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Hootsuite Certification Assignment
