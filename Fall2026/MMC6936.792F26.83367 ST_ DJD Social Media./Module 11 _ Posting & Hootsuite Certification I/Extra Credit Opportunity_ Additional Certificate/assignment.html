Extra Credit Opportunity: Additional Certificate

For extra credit (20 points), you are asked to complete either one of "Hootsuite Platform Certification" or "Social Listening with Hootsuite".

  Assignment Instructions 

1. Signup For Free

Sign up for free using the same steps as Social Media Marketing Certification. 

 

2. Take The Certificate Exam

Once you have completed the course modules and quizzes, take the certification exam. You have 3 free attempts.

This exam is very, very detailed. Unless you're a professional at using Hootsuite Dashboard, it's very difficult to take the exam without any preparation.

To prepare:

  1. Open a Hootsuite account at https://hootsuite.com/.Links to an external site.
  2. Explore the Hootsuite Dashboard, link your social media accounts, experiment with publishing content from Hootsuite to social sites
  3. On Hootsuite Academy, complete the Hootsuite Platform course. TAKE NOTES during this course.

The questions on the exam will be very detailed such as:

  • Which features appear in the message compose window?
  • Which features are NOT part of the metrics report?
  • Features available in the mobile version of Hootsuite
  • How to automate blog posts into social media posts
  • Questions about metrics and analytics reports, which aren't even part of the free version of the Hootsuite Dashboard

Most of the answers to the exam questions are covered and explained in the course. You will have to listen very carefully to the course and do all the readings by taking notes.

I'm going to say you can all take the exam one time/the first time to get an idea of the level of detail in the question. Then, if you don't pass, you can go back to the course and take it again while taking notes on the questions you missed on the exam. Then you can try again.

The Hootsuite Platform Certification tests to see how well you know the Hootsuite Dashboard interface and features.

    • You have 60 minutes to complete the exam.
    • The exam has 50 multiple-choice questions.
    • You need 75% or more to pass and receive the certification.
    • You have three free attempts, after which you must pay the $99 fee if you still desire to have this certification.
    • You will receive a certification (as a PDF) and the opportunity to be listed in Hootsuite's directory of certified professionals.

 

3. Turn it In

 When you pass the certification exam, Hootsuite will email you your certificate. Same as the main certification assignment, you are asked to acquire this certificate within this semester. To submit for grading, upload that certificate here.

The deadline: for this certification is 11: 59 p.m. on November 26. No late work or partial grade is accepted for extra credits.
