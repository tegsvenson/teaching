USF Course Evaluation

This assignment is created to encourage you to participate in the Student Assessment of Instruction. You must have received a message from USF about course evaluation, the online eXplorance Blue assessment system.

I am always happy to learn what students experienced in my course, whether it was good or bad. Providing an optimal learning experience is always my first priority, and I am always looking to improve. What you provide will be a huge resource for my pursuit of teaching effectiveness. Thank you in advance for your participation.

Your evaluation of this course is also important as it provides precious information about improving teaching for the University of South Florida. Your input will help us improve teaching and provide a better learning experience for future students. This will improve and maintain the quality of education in USF, which, in turn, will benefit you as well.

This is USF's official course evaluation, and I do not have access to any personal identifier. Your comments and feedback will remain anonymous. This assignment was created following USF Academic Affairs' recommendations.

  Assignment Instructions 

Extra Credit Policy:

  • All members of the class will be awarded 15 points to their final grade if 80% of the enrolled class completes evaluations; and 25 points if more than 95% of the enrolled class completes evaluations.
  • To complete this assignment, please finish the evaluation in the system, then upload a screenshot confirming you completed the course evaluation.
