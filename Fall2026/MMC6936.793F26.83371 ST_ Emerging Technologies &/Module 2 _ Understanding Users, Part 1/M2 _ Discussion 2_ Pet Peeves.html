M2 _ Discussion 2_ Pet Peeves

Module 2 | Discussion: Pet Peeves 

In this discussion board post, you will discuss their negative experience with interactive media. You will cover one of the ideas provided to you through the module lectures or readings. There are two deadlines for this (and most other) discussions.

 Discussion Instructions

In this module's reading, we have learned about the design principles that interface designers frequently use: 

  • Visibility 
  • Feedback 
  • Constraints 
  • Consistency 
  • Affordance 

Think about an interactive interface that you frequently use. For example, it can range from an app on your smartphone, a remote controller, to even a gas pump. Please describe in detail one pet peeve that you have about it. It does not have to be something too serious; it can be something that just mildly irritates you. Ideally, it should be something that happens routinely.

Then, apply one of the design principles above and briefly explain why that applies. This part of this discussion is due Thursday, September 4, 11:59 PM.

Respond to Your Classmates

Then, respond to one of your classmate’s experiences. Either share a different experience that you had with the same interface or a similar experience that you had with a different interface. Then, choose one of the usability goals that apply to your classmate’s experience. Provide a rationale for why this goal applies to the experience. For full credit, you need to provide your own analytical view in your response using the constructs below. 

  • Efficiency 
  • Utility 
  • Learnability 
  • Memorability 
  • Safety 

Responding to your classmates is due Saturday, September 6, 11:59 PM

Note 1. A correct application of design principles and usability goals and a rationale are required for full credit.
