Module 11 | Assignment 9: Report Posts

Submit Your Posts

For the last few days, you have been publishing content on your group brand's social media accounts. You should have screenshots of all your work saved somewhere, as well as the original files, when relevant. 

  Assignment Instructions 

1. Complete Your Post

Collect the screenshots and files of all 11 (or more) of your posts from the last few weeks across your brand's social media platforms. (This includes your archived Livestream). Then, embed them in Word documents, Canva files, etc., and submit a single PDF file.

 

2. Review

Under each screenshot, summarize:

  • Working link to the each post
  • Where the post was published
  • Date and time of publication
  • What format was it published in (e.g., posts, reels, shorts, etc.)
  • How it performed (views, likes, comments, shares, etc.)
  • Additionally, review the rubric for grading.

 

3. Turn it In

Upload the PDF file that includes the information above. 

Deadline: You have until 11:59 pm on November 26. Note that this is for submitting the collection of all of your posts after the campaign is over. The date and time for individual posts on their platforms should follow your editorial calendar.
