Module 6 Overview

We concentrate on finalizing your ideas on the I.D.E.A. Project. Instead of a set of readings and lectures, the instructor and the student will have 1-on-1 meetings to review their ideas from the Proposal and finalize the ideas, so that everyone can move on to creating prototypes.

 

 Module Learning Outcome

Upon successful completion of this module, you will be able to:

  1. Identify how users can communicate more affectedly based on recipient communication
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Assignment
  2. Comprehend the core psychological underpinnings and its concepts related to user experience in emerging media technologies.
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Assignment
