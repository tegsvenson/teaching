Module 4 | Lesson 5: Social Network

  Lectures

The following lecture provides a broad overview on how social relationships and connections can be visualized and analyzed, so we can have a better understanding about how social connection on social media is shaped.

 Readings

   Chapter 1: Introduction of The Oxford handbook of social networks Book

Introduces key concepts regarding social media network analysis.
Light, Ryan, and James W. Moody, eds. The Oxford Handbook of Social Networks. New York: Oxford University Press, 2020. Print.

 

Optional Reading:

  Chapter 2: Graph Theory and Social Networks of Networks, crowds, and markets: reasoning about a highly connected world Book

Provides the basics regarding how social networks can be visualized.
Easley, David, and Jon Kleinberg. Networks, Crowds, and Markets : Reasoning About a Highly Connected World. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2010. Print.
