Module 4 | Lesson 6: Social Media Audit

  Lecture

When we use the term "audit" in marketing contexts for businesses and brands, we're referring to either one of two things:

  • An internal audit involves taking a look inside the brand and analyzing your social metrics. You can read more about this on the Hootsuite Blog
  • Links to an external site. if you're interested; we'll be revisiting an internal audit/analysis at the end of the semester. 
  • An external audit, also known as a competitive analysis, is taking a look at all of the outside competition for your brand.

We are doing a little of both to get your social media brands started.
