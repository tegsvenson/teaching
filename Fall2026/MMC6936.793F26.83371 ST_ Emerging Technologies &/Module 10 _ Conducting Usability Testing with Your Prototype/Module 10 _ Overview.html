Module 10 Overview

You are now working on the core idea of the assignment series in this course: Usability testing. In part 7, you can elect to go over your work with me. In part 8, you will prepare and conduct your usability test, and submit the recordings from your experience with tester. In part 9, you will work on coming up with a report on the objective and subjective findings that derived from the tests.

 Module Learning Outcome

Upon successful completion of this module, you will be able to:

  1. Identify key emerging technologies that are related to communication.
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Project Part 7; (2) Project Part 8; (3) Project Part 9
  2. Demonstrate understanding of the user/receiver perspectives of communication to communicate with others more effectively.
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Project Part 7; (2) Project Part 8; (3) Project Part 9
  3. Demonstrate understanding of the practical concepts that are related to user experience.
    • Assignments and activities used to assess Module Learning Outcome: (1) Project Part 7; (2) Project Part 8; (3) Project Part 9
