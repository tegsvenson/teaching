Dr. Stephen W. Song is an assistant professor in the Department of Journalism and Digital Communication. He teaches classes in Visual Literacy, Social Media, and Food and Communication. His research interest is in the application of psychological theories on new and emerging communication technologies. Specifically, he is interested in understanding the effects of interactive technologies, such as artificial intelligence and virtual reality, on users’ trust in journalism and communication.
