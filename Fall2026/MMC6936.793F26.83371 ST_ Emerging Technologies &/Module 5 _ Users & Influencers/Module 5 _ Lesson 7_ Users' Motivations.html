 Readings

  Chapter 17: Uses and Gratifications of Social Media: Who Uses It and Why? of The Oxford Handbook of Cyberpsychology Book

An overview of why and how people use social media in general
Atrill-Smith, Alison, et al., editors. The Oxford Handbook of Cyberpsychology. Oxford University Press, 2018.

  One Size Doesn’t Fit All: A Uses and Gratifications Analysis of Social Media Platforms, Journal Article USF Library Link

Explores how user motivations differ depending on the service that each social media platform provides
Pelletier, Mark J., et al. “One Size Doesn’t Fit All: a Uses and Gratifications Analysis of Social Media Platforms.” Journal of Research in Interactive Marketing., vol. 14, no. 2, 2020, pp. 269–84, https://doi.org/10.1108/JRIM-10-2019-0159.
