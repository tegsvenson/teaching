Module 5 Overview

In this module, we first focus on understanding how and why individuals use social media from Uses & Gratification perspective. We then take a closer look on an interesting entity that was introduced to the world as social media became more popular - influencers. Reflecting on what we learned about users' motivations and how influencers entice their audience, we then apply these concepts on pinpointing our brand's audiences and their personas in Assignment 2. 

  Objectives
