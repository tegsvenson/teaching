 Assignment Instructions

This project description is the final version of your proposal. 

In the project description, describe the following: 

  • All the requirements in the proposal.
    • The title of your design alternative
    • Whether it is a completely new application or a feature within an existing application
    • The existing app, function, or content that you will compare your design alternative against.
    • User task: a step-by-step description of the user's task. You will use this for usability testing.
    • Application of Theory
      • Provide a prediction of user experience based on theory.
      • The theoretical concept should come from usability goals or design principles.
      • The concept should be clearly related to the user task, which is the function that you are implementing in the app.
    • Feasibility: Specify the tool that you will use. Make sure these are promises that you can keep. Your subsequent assignments will be graded based on the information in this assignment.
  • Summary of the findings from the Interview, and how they are incorporated, or not incorporated. Explain clearly the rationale for your decision.
  • Address the agendas that were raised in the Meeting. You will receive all the points that you lost in Assignment 1 in the form of extra credits by successfully revising and finalizing your ideas in this assignment. Late work penalties are not included.
