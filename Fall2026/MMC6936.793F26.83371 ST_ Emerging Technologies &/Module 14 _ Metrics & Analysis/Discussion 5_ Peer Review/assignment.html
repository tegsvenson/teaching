  Discussion Board Instructions 

Post a Link

We are going back to the discussion board that we used to propose and pick a brand for your Social Media Project. Everyone shared their link to each brand's social media page. 

Peer Review

    1. You will review the brand that you proposed. As the brand owner, you are reviewing your social media manager's work.
    2. Wait till the posting period is over for the class. Send me an email (song100@usf.edu) if you don't have access to the brand's website by December 9.
    3. Go through their content, and summarize what you saw in a few sentences.
    4. Find one element in your classmate's content that can be useful if applied to your content, and explain why and how it can improve yours. This element should be missing in your own project. Focus on techniques, writing, or planning.
    5. Find one element that is not in #2, that is something that you did not expect when you proposed your brand. This should be something positive and seemingly works for the brand. Explain the reason why you think it works and why you find this unexpected.
    6. Find one element that is not in #3, that is something subpar compared to your expectation as a brand owner. Explain why and how you would have done that.
    7. Finally, choose one post that is most interesting from your project, and provide a link to it. Explain why you think that post is most interesting and meaningful.

Submit the Peer Review document that you created to this assignment prompt, using either PDF or DOCX file. The word count is less than 800 words.
