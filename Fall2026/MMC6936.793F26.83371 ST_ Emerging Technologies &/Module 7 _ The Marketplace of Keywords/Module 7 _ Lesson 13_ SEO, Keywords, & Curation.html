Module 7 | Lesson 13: SEO, Keywords, & Curation

With our knowledge of how attention works and how it's related to engagement and, ultimately, recommendations on social media; we are utilizing SEO. SEO stands for "Search Engine Optimization," which is essentially what you can do to your brand's online presence to make it more discoverable by search engines. Because this class is focused on social media, we will be studying this in the form of keywords and hashtags.

Keywords are a way to accomplish search engine optimization. Utilizing keywords in content and hashtags increases a post’s ability to reach audiences organically. 

Hashtags usually come after the caption of choice under each picture or video. They are identifying tags attached to a post that helps users search and find content in a streamlined way. 

Content curation is the practice of curating content related to your brand across social platforms.
