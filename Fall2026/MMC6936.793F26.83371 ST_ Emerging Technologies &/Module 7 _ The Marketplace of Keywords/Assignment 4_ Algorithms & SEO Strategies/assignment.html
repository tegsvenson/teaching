Module 7 | Assignment 4: Working with Algorithms

Now that you have a basic understanding of how algorithms work, we are going to strategize on how to overcome them and/or use them to our advantage. 

  Assignment Instructions 

1. Login to Canva

Once you've logged into Canva, open up this template file for this assignment. Don't forget to personalize the template with your brand's logo, colors, and fonts. 

 

2. Algorithms Reach

Conduct research on the algorithms for your brand's two core platforms (although you will post on only one of them, pick two here so we can practice). Make sure you use authoritative sources and, if possible, the most up-to-date (later than 2021 ONLY) information. Some good websites to try are Hootsuite, Sprout Social, and even the social platform "about" pages. Keep track of the sources you use so you can credit them accordingly.

Report what you found. Once you've researched your brand's two core platforms' algorithms, in the Canva template, answer the following questions [for each core platform]:

  1. How does the algorithm for this core platform function?
    • Provide at least one source here.
  2. According to 2-A, how will you organize and prioritize posts and content?
  3. According to 2-A, what are some strategies that you can use when you create content for your brand to use the algorithm to your advantage?
  4. The two platforms likely will serve separate functions. Based on the previous findings in previous assignments, explain how they will be differently used.

 

3. Pick out Keywords and Hashtags for your Posts

Follow along the instructions in the template. Specifically:

  • Go to https://www.semrush.com/. This is a keyword tool; some aspects of the tool require premium access. For this assignment, you only are required to use the free tool. (If you are blocked from seeing data on certain keywords from this tool, simply try typing another keyword). If a different tool or website is working for you, you may choose that over Semrush.
  • Start searching by entering potential keywords for your brand by using them as keywords on Semrush. Typing in keywords will direct you to the Keyword Overview page for the search term. If not, click the hamburger menu icon (☰) and click "Keyword Overview."
  • The overall "Volume" is the information that you want. The higher the number, the more frequent that word is used as a search term in search engines.
  • Make a list of the most prominent keywords and their respective posts/search volumes. Make sure you screen capture or export the search results that you will use for this assignment.
  • Then, based on those keywords, make a list of hashtags that will be relevant to your posts. You can base your hashtags on your insight from the keywords tool, on research from Hootsuite and other sources, and on suggested tags and recommended searches from social engines. 
  • Finally, add your unique hashtags and provide a rationale.

 

4. Turn it in

When you have completed the assignment, (1) export the page from Canva as a PDF, compile and submit it with your earlier assignments, and (2) leave a comment on your assignment's link.
