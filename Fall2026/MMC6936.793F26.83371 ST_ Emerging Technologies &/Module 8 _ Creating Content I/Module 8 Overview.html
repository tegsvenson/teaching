Module 8: Overview

This module marks the beginning of the content creation and preparation phase of social media marketing campaign. We will start with creating a set of ethical and legal guidelines for our campaign in Assignment 5.

  Objectives

By the end of this unit, you will:

  • MO1: Summarize how to uphold social media content to traditional journalistic and communication norms and ethics, such as fact-checking, verification, accuracy, objectivity, and ethics
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives  (1) Assignments
  • MO2: Demonstrate the best methods for creating content on social media, including live streaming, curating content, producing multimedia, promoting brands, and sharing news.
    • Assignments and learning activities used to assess Module Objectives (1) Assignments)
