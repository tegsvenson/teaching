Module 3 | Discussion 3: Pick Your Brand

In this discussion, we are choosing the brand that each of you will work on throughout this semester.

 Assignment Instructions 

1. Pick Your Brand

  • We will work on this assignment by revisiting the last module's discussion, where you created and proposed your brand.
  • Once you are there, leave a comment on one of the brands that your classmates have proposed.
  • You must choose a brand that is not created by yourself.
    • Why do this? Ideating and creating a brand/product/service is closer to entrepreneurship, rather than marketing. Historically, there have been many successful entrepreneurs who have also been very good at marketing. However, this class focuses on marketing. Most marketers work for brands that other people have created. Still, marketers often have some extent of control over which brand they work for. Therefore, by reassigning ourselves to a brand that we did not create (but still choose what to do), we are distancing ourselves from the idea of entrepreneurship and focusing more on the marketing aspect of the brand.
  • First-come, first-served: The first commenter for each brand gets to work on the brand
    • Why do this? This is the way that will maximize fairness in brand selection for everyone. My arbitrary assignment will not be much pleasurable for you either. And if you don't, it might be more interesting. Many people end up happily working on projects that they initially did not expect to do.
    • Timeline: Commenting to secure your project starts at 6:00 a.m., September 8. You have till 11:59 p.m., September 14, to leave a comment.
    • If there is already a comment on a brand expressing an intention to work on it for the project, consider that the brand is taken. Move on to a different brand that has not received a comment yet.

2. Turn It In:

Once you have finished commenting and chosen your brand, come back to this assignment and submit the following:

  • Name of the brand
  • Description of the brand, provided by the creator
  • Full name of the student who created the brand
