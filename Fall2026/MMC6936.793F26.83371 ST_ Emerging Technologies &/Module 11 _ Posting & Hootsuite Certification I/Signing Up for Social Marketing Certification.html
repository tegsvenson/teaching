Singing Up for Social Marketing Certification

Earning the Social Marketing Certification by from Hootsuite this semester is a requirement in this class. It accounts for a significant amount of your grade. I am not in any way affiliated with the company. Still, they provide a good education experience for free, and at the end, you can earn a certification which you can add to your résumé.

2. Available Courses

Once Hootsuite grants you access to their student program, they will provide you with access to:

  • Hootsuite Academy courseware for Hootsuite Platform (Hootsuite Platform Training)
  • Free Hootsuite Platform Certification ($99 value)
  • Free Social Marketing Course ($199 value)
  • Free Social Marketing Certification ($199 value)
  • Hootsuite dashboard (core features)

 

3. Requirements

 Among the above, below are the requirements and Extra Credit Opportunities:

  • Social Marketing Certification: Requirement
  • Hootsuite Platform Certification: Extra Credit Opportunity

Note. The certifications must be dated within this semester for you to receive grades for your work. If you already have the certifications, consult me ASAP at song100@usf.edu.
