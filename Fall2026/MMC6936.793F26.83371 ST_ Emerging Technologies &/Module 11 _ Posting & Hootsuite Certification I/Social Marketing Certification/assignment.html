Hootsuite Social Media Marketing Certification

 This is the area to turn in your certification evidence for 10% of your grade. Here is more information about the certificate in this course.

  Assignment Instructions 

1. Signup For Free

Hootsuite normally charges money for these certifications; however, as part of our partnership with Hootsuite, you get to take the exams for free. PLEASE DO NOT PAY, GIVE OUT CREDIT CARD, OR SIGN UP FOR A FREE TRIAL. If you're on a page that asks for payment or a credit card, you're in the wrong place. To sign up for free, follow this guide.

 

2. Take The Course

You'll want to complete the course modules and quizzes within the next 3 weeks. I will publish a recommended schedule, but there is no restriction here. If you want to work ahead, that is completely fine. However, make sure you don't fall behind so that you can take the certification exam before the required deadline. 

 

3. Take The Certificate Exame

 Once you have completed the course modules and quizzes, take the certification exam. You have 3 free attempts and must score at least an 80%. 

 

4. Turn it In

 When you pass the certification exam, Hootsuite will email you your certificate. To submit for grading, please upload that certificate here. 

 

Note: You have until 11:59 p.m. on November 26 to turn in evidence that you have completed the Hootsuite Certification. 

 

  What if I have my Hootsuite Social Media Certification already?

If you have completed the Hootsuite Social Marketing certification for a previous class, you have an alternate assignment option:

ALTERNATE ASSIGNMENT OPTION 2: LINKEDIN CERTIFICATION 

 

1. Email Me

To start, email me your Hootsuite Social Marketing certification so that I can verify you qualify for the alternate assignment.

 

2. Login To LinkedIn Learning

 Log into my.usf.edu. Then, in the Learning & Teaching Tools dropdown, select Online Training (LinkedIn Learning). You may be prompted to log in with your USF email.

    • NOTE: If you have a separate LinkedIn account, make sure you log in with your USF email. This will give you free access to the learning courses. 

 

3. Find The Course

 Use the search bar to find the LinkedIn Learning course. You can choose one of the following:

    • Develop Your Social Media Marketing Skills: This one might be too easy/redundant.
    • Social Media Marketing Trends: This is more advanced (and labeled intermediate). Advanced is not equal to better, however. Concepts here are very up-to-date, but some of them are a bit too trendy and may become obsolete soon.

 

4. Finish The Course & Earn The Certificate

 Once you go through the entire course, LinkedIn will award you with a certificate of completion.

 

5. Trun it In

 When you earn your certificate of completion, upload that certificate here for grading.

Note: You have until  11:59 pm on November 26 to turn in evidence that you have completed one of these two alternate assignment options. Please note you may only do these alternate assignments if you already have your Hootsuite Certification Completed.
