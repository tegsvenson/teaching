Module 1 Discussion Board

Social Media Introduction

  Discussion Board Instructions 

Instructions:

  1. Visual Introduction: I want you to introduce yourself in a visual format. This is to start immediately preparing you to create content for your social media posts in this class. You can use any software you like: PowerPoint, Word, Adobe programs, Canva, Video Editing, etc., so long as it is a visual post. 
  2. Include this information: whether it's a part of your design/video or typed out in the post below the design, you must include the following in your introduction.
      • Name: Your name or nickname 
      • Strengths: What are your strengths? What content creation experience do you have? Do you have an online portfolio you want to share?
      • Weaknesses: What are your weaknesses? Be honest here. Do you have trouble procrastinating? Will you need groupmates who can help you work ahead so you don't do that?
      • Interests:  What are your interests? Do you have any ideas already for a potential project?
  3. Post and reply to at least one classmate. 
    1. Leave a comment to a classmate of your choice.
    2. If possible, try to pick a classmate who has not received a comment.
    3. Greet each other!

Deadline: You have until 11:59 pm on August 28 to post your visual introduction and your reply to a classmate. I recommend posting your visual introduction early, so others have enough time to see it and reply.  

Tip 1:

  • If you have no experience designing anything in online software yet, that's okay! You can easily record a video of yourself right in Canvas. To do so, hit "reply," click the video icon, and use your computer's webcam and mic to record a video. You can read more instructions on how to record media in a discussion here. Links to an external site.
  • Here is how you embed an image into your reply: How to embed an image into a discussion post in CanvasLinks to an external site.
  • I also recommend signing up for a Canva account (Links to an external site.). Canva is a free online tool that you can use to create designs. (You are not required to purchase the premium subscription for this class). To use Canva, go to https://www.canva.com/ (Links to an external site.) and create an account/sign in. Then press Create a Design in the top right corner; it will ask what size you want to use-- any size is fine. From there you can choose templates, elements, etc., to add to your design. You will have to Download your design as a JPEG file to embed into your Canvas reply. 
  • Read the rubric to see how you will be graded on this assignment. 
  • Have fun and be creative!
